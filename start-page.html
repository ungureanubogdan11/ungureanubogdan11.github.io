The Movies

Cars (2006)

While on his way to a major race, rookie racer Lightning McQueen gets lost and ends up in the small town of Radiator Springs, where he meets quirky characters and learns that winning isn't the most important thing.

Cars 2 (2011)

Lightning McQueen and Mater go overseas for the World Grand Prix, but their plans are disrupted when Mater accidentally gets involved in international espionage.

Cars 3 (2017)

Blindsided by a new generation of blazing-fast racers, the legendary Lightning McQueen is suddenly pushed out of the sport he loves. To get back in the game, he'll need help from an eager young race technician, Cruz Ramirez.

Meet the Residents of Radiator Springs

Lightning McQueen

#95. A Piston Cup champion who learned to slow down and appreciate the friends he made along the way.

Mater

The lovable, rusty tow-truck and Lightning's best friend. He's the heart of Radiator Springs.

Doc Hudson

The "Fabulous Hudson Hornet." A legendary racer turned town judge and doctor, who became Lightning's mentor.

Sally Carrera

A smart and charming Porsche who left the fast lane to become the town's attorney and proprietor of the Cozy Cone Motel.
